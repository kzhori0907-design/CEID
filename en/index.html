Creating the future of education, together.

The Innovation Co-creation Center brings universities, companies, and local governments together — connecting cutting-edge expertise in AI, data science, and engineering with education to make schools and society more engaging and enriching.

About

About the Center

The Industry-Government-Academia Innovation Co-creation Center integrates the resources of universities, industry, and government to create innovative forms of education. The Center also manages shared scientific equipment on campus, trains primary and secondary school teachers with strong capabilities in science and technology education, and contributes to advancing science education in schools and local communities.
