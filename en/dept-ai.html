Mission

Mission

The Education AI and Data Science Co-creation Division uses AI and educational data to address challenges in education and create new forms of learning. Universities, companies, local governments and schools collaborate to develop and implement AI-based educational support, school support tools, educational content and educational programs.

Activities

Activities

  • Development and school-based implementation of AI educational support and school support tools
  • Development of educational content based on the collection and analysis of educational data
  • Planning and implementation of evidence-based educational programs in collaboration with universities, companies, local governments and schools
