Overview

Overview

Under the Cabinet Office’s BRIDGE program, this project develops and conducts demonstration research on AI agents for education. In collaboration with the National Institute of Informatics and other partners, the project advances research and development with a view to implementation in educational settings.

Activities

Activities

  • Research and development of AI agents for education
  • Demonstration research in school settings
  • Transferring research outcomes to educational practice and society
