Mission

Mission

The Science and Next-generation Education Division researches and implements education for developing the next generation through science education, STEAM education and inquiry-based learning. Universities, schools, companies and local governments collaborate to develop and test teaching materials and educational programs, while also providing opportunities for children and students to engage in inquiry and hands-on creation.

Activities

Activities

  • Development and demonstration of teaching materials and educational programs in science education, STEAM education and inquiry-based learning
  • Providing opportunities for children and students to engage in inquiry and hands-on creation
  • Supporting the development of teachers with advanced capabilities in science and technology education
