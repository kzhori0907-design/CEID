Mission

Mission

The Industry–Government–Academia Co-creation Facilitation Division connects universities, companies, government agencies and schools and develops partnerships into concrete initiatives. Through education-focused university research administrators (eRAs), the Division coordinates collaborative research, teaching-material development, demonstration projects, training and seminars to address diverse educational challenges and create new value.

Activities

Activities

  • Coordinating industry–government–academia collaboration through education-focused university research administrators (eRAs)
  • Planning and accompanying collaborative research, teaching-material development and demonstration projects
  • Supporting solutions to educational challenges and new value creation through training, seminars and related initiatives
